Rotoscope

Motion

I had a hard time picking a subject to film for the rotoscope project, but after church while I was waiting, I saw two children playing around and thought to myself I should film them. When I was watching through the video, I thought back to my childhood. Childhood is a time where there's no worries about the future, a time where we focused on playing, making friends, and wondering what's for snack or lunch time. Sometimes we take the smallest things in life for granted.
