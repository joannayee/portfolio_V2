Happea

Branding

Happea is a spa care package company that helps young women–18-30–who feels down in the dumps; mentally and emotionally, and need a pick-me up. Happea believe in actively uplifting young womens’ spirit through aromatic and energy-boosting products.

  • Year: 2019
  • Medium: Digital
  • Role: Sole/principle Designer
  • Software: Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign

Color

Colors are the essence of the soul. They have the ability to convey emotions and meanings. I chose the colors with bright and joyful undertones to create an uplifting atmosphere.

Brand Graphics

I designed fun and joyful graphics to enhance. I went through trial and error to prefect the illustrations so that the graphic don't seem too clip-art like.

Packaging

I designed the box with an online printing service. During the drafting process, I encountered some problems, I thought I could use cardboard to make my own inserts. After I tested out the cardboard, I did some research and looked for foam to use instead. I found some cushion foam and cut out holes for my products.
